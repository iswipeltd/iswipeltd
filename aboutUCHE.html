About Us

The Best IT Solution With 10 Years of Experience

I-Swipe Tech is a multifaceted tech company that has proven track records in digital marketing, advertising, software development, and the provision of innovative and creative tech solutions to our clients. We are a fast-rising brand aimed at turning other brands, whatever their niche into global successes. We have been successful in creating much-needed visibility for our array of clients with sustainable services delivered by our professional team. Our mission is to create the most compelling Digital Marketing and Advertising company of the 21st century in Africa, while providing top-notch tech innovations, making our services sustainable, and building a creative and globally diverse team. At I-Swipe Tech, we believe the digital space is big enough for us all. We make it our duty to give you and your brand your place in it to thrive and succeed. We have also launched our ICT Academy which is aimed at equipping the youths with top-rated tech skills relevant to today's world. This is directly in line with our vision which is: "To accelerate and innovate digital and Tech solutions in Africa."
